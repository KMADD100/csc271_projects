Spam Musubi

Spam musubi is a popular lunch food in Hawaii, made with grilled Spam on top of a block of rice, wrapped together with seaweed.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Spam 1 can
Cooked Rice 2 cups
Seaweed 1 sheets
Soy Sauce 1 tbsp
